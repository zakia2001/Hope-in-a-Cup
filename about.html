About Us

Hope in a Cup is more than just a place to savor delightful flavors; it's a testament to social responsibility and empowerment. As a non-profit organization sponsored by Synopsys for Good, and established under the Entrepreneurship Development Program, we take pride in creating a welcoming space for everyone.

Our Mission

At Hope in a Cup, we are driven by a mission to foster inclusivity and provide employment opportunities for the LGBTQ+ community. We believe in the power of diversity, and through our cafe, we strive to create a space where individuals from all walks of life feel embraced and celebrated.

Why Choose Us

Whether you're here for a cup of coffee, a delicious meal, or simply to be part of our inclusive community, we welcome you. Our cafe is more than just a place; it's a movement towards positive change. Join us in this journey of empowerment and inclusion.
